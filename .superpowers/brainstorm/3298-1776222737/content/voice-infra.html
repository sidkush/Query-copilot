My recommendation: 5 (Hybrid Tiered). Whisper local as default means 95% of your users get free, private, instant voice without you paying a cent. Deepgram kicks in for power users who live in voice. OpenAI Realtime turns on for Stage Mode demos so executives get the full conversational voice OUT magic. Same architecture as your existing BYOK provider system — three substrates, one interface. Engineering cost is real but every other approach forces a single tradeoff that locks out a customer segment.
