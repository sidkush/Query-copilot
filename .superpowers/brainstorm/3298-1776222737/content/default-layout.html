My recommendation: B (Bottom Dock) as the daily-driver default. Reasons: maximum chart real estate (the chart IS the product), familiar Claude/ChatGPT-style input dock at bottom, agent step-pills give just-enough transparency without eating canvas space. C (Layered Float) is the right pattern for Stage Mode (it natively supports the floating-orb-and-bubble feel for HUD themes). A (Side-by-Side) stays available as a Pro Mode toggle for analysts who want full chat history visible.

Pick one, click multi-select if you want a hybrid, or describe in the terminal if none feels right and I'll iterate. Note: voice mic is shown as always-visible here, but the actual flow (push-to-talk vs always-listening vs wake word) is the next question.
