  The Big Picture: What Makes QueryCopilot Win

  The council converges on a three-layer differentiation strategy:

  1. Foundation (Week 1): Fix broken elements + parallel perf + auto-save = trust & speed
  2. Showpiece (Week 2): Presentation Engine with auto-layout = "better than Tableau" moment
  3. AI Moat (Week 3+): Conversational editing + anomaly narrator + NL alerts = things no traditional BI tool can do

  The AI capabilities are QueryCopilot's unfair advantage — Tableau and Power BI can't easily add conversational editing or anomaly narration because they lack an LLM at the core.
