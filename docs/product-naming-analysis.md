# Product Naming Analysis — QueryCopilot Rebrand

**Date:** 2026-04-06
**Context:** Evolving from "QueryCopilot" (NL-to-SQL copilot) to a full agentic data intelligence platform where AI agents build, edit, and manage dashboards autonomously. Competing with Tableau, Power BI, Looker.

---

## Original Query

> Now as we have moved towards agentic dashboard experience for the users, where the agents can even build and edit the dashboard at next level and make it full agentic experience in the future. "QueryCopilot" doesn't seem to suit the taste. Suggest 10 different most relevant product names for this product. Think about this deeply and from business perspective for easy monetization. Corporate, formal, professional as this tool will be a competitor to tools like Tableau, Power BI and Looker.

---

## 10 Product Name Candidates

### 1. Tessera
*Latin: "mosaic tile"* — Each dashboard tile is a tessera; together they form the complete picture. Directly maps to the product's tile-based architecture. Short, elegant, globally pronounceable. Positions as: "The AI that assembles your data mosaic."

### 2. Axion
*Greek: "worthy, fundamental truth"* — Implies the platform surfaces the foundational truths hidden in data. Scientific, authoritative, two syllables. Works across languages. Positions as: "From raw data to axiomatic insight."

### 3. Luminari
*Latin: lumen (light) + ari (belonging to)* — "Those who illuminate." Premium, almost luxury-tier naming. Natural upgrade path: Luminari Free > Luminari Pro > Luminari Enterprise. Positions as: "Illuminate every database."

### 4. Clareo
*Latin: "I make clear"* — First-person verb — the AI speaks: "I clarify your data." Two syllables, razor-sharp brand promise. Rhymes with nothing in the BI space (differentiation). Positions as: "Ask anything. Clareo answers."

### 5. Meridian
*The prime reference line in navigation* — Evokes precision, global scale, finding your bearings. Already carries corporate weight (finance, consulting use it). Works as "Meridian Analytics" or standalone. Positions as: "Your data's true north."

### 6. Syntera
*Synthesis + era* — Signals a new era of data synthesis. The "syn-" prefix (together) reflects unifying 18 databases. Modern, tech-forward, trademarkable. Positions as: "Every database. One intelligence."

### 7. Vantage
*A superior point of observation* — Immediately communicates the value proposition: see more, see better. One word, universally understood, boardroom-ready. "Vantage AI" or "Vantage Analytics" for disambiguation. Positions as: "The vantage point your data deserves."

### 8. Athenix
*Athena (goddess of wisdom & strategy) + ix (tech suffix)* — Intellectual authority without being academic. The "-ix" gives it a modern SaaS edge (like Datadog, Splunk). Positions as: "Strategic intelligence, autonomously delivered."

### 9. Signum
*Latin: "sign, signal, mark"* — The AI finds the signals in the noise. Short, strong, scientific. Works beautifully with "Signum AI." The plural "Signa" could name the agent system. Positions as: "Every signal. Every database. One platform."

### 10. Auria
*Latin: aurum (gold)* — Mining golden insights from raw data. Premium, aspirational, three syllables with a soft ending that sounds approachable. Natural pricing language: "Go gold with Auria Pro." Positions as: "The gold standard of data intelligence."

## Top 3 Recommendation (for monetization + category creation)

1. **Tessera** — Most unique, directly tied to the tile/dashboard architecture, zero confusion with existing brands, and the "mosaic" metaphor scales from "one tile" (free) to "complete masterpiece" (enterprise).

2. **Luminari** — Strongest premium positioning. The name *sounds* expensive, which anchors higher pricing psychology. "Luminari Enterprise" sounds like it costs $200/seat and should.

3. **Clareo** — Simplest, sharpest brand promise. One word that IS the value proposition. Easy to verb-ify: "Just Clareo it."
